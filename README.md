# JEC Flow Service Platform

- ✅ Phone/Email registration and login
- ✅ User information management
- ✅ Login state persistence

### 2. Flow Experience
- ✅ Boost Your Mood - Flow Container

### 3. AI Voice Companion

- ✅ Auto interruption and resumption

### 4. Professional Services
- ✅ Sports Social Flow Science
- ✅ Highlight Moments Neuroscience
- ✅ Career Flow Neuroscience
- ✅ Complete order and payment system
